I was playing a war videogame commanding units in a cave at the bottom of three-storey concrete seating with a staircase in the middle. There was an enemy flamethrower unit blocked the exit so I built a mini barracks on the stairs and created a sniper who shot at them multiple times but did no damage. I created 10 machine gunners who took cover behind the top step in a line and fired simultaneously at them which made them flee.

We travelled to distant enemy trenches and I built a mini tank factory to create tanks to attack the trenches by firing rounds inside but they were slow to reload and did no damage. I created a flamethrower unit who ran down curvy steps into the trenches and fired a stream of flames at packed enemy soldiers which proved effective at spreading the flames and making them flee.
