# Description

I was standing in the lounge room of my unit as my dad sat on the sofa when I looked through the blinds outside to see that everything was flooded by 0.5 meters of murky water.

> **Me**: Everything is flooded outside and the washing is still on the line!
> 

I was worried about how we were going to get the clothes inside without dropping them in the water. However, when exiting into the backyard all of the water was already absorbed back into the ground with droplets scattered on the bricks.

> **Me**: Where did the water go?
> 

> **Dad**: That’s just a normal thing that happens during the summer.
> 

> **Me**: Let’s the clothes in quickly while we have this opportunity because we don’t know if the water will return.
> 

We both hurriedly placed all of the clothes in a basket and my dad carried inside.
