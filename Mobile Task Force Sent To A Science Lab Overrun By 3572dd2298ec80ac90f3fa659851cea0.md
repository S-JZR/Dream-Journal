# Description

I was in a videogame as one of many mobile task force members waiting in line to be sent through a teleporter to a science facility overrun by four monsters to restore order. I stood in a white room on a platform that lifted me to a white circular device on the ceiling with a dark hole in the centre. I closed my eyes to avoid seeing unpleasant things and brace myself to experience momentary pain and brief unconsciousness as my body dissolved into blue particles, travelled through a network, and reassembled.

After briefly exploring, everyone was being killed so quickly that we split up to survive on our own. I built a speed boost pad in a side room and when a monster approached me I used it to quickly flee away. However, I couldn’t outrun another fast monster that killed me by stabbing me in the back. I was a spirit stuck in a grey void forever until others came along but I came back to life. Large blobs of alien flesh were gradually growing to fill the entire facility so those who remained evacuated by jumping through a teleporter mounted to the entrance wall on mass.
