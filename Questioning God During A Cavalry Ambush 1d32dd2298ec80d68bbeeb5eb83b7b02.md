I was one of many soldiers crossing a mostly flat grassy field towards a forest when distant enemy gunfire erupted, prompting everyone to sprint forward and drop into a prone position behind small mounds of dirt. Incoming bullets struck the ground nearby, kicking up puffs of dust into the air as I feared being shot in the head. I couldn't believe that God allowed our lives to be like this and I was forced to fight in combat. A wave of cavalry charged at us from the trees 100 meters ahead.

> **Man**: Calvary ahead!

> **Man**: Present!
> 

> **Man**: Fire!

We fired a volley but most of our shots seemed to miss as they continued to gallop passed us.
