# Description

I was standing outside my unit at night when I saw hundreds of white lights moving in the sky across the street and called out to my mum.

Me: Come look outside, the sky is full of weird lights!

The shot changed to a close up of the lights, revealing they were alien dark grey pyramid-shaped drones with a glowing blue circle eye on the front face. All the drones merged together into various mini spaceships.

A giant mothership, resembling an imperial Star Destroyer from Star Wars with red stripes on the side and five landing bays on top, flew over my unit complex. The mini spaceships rapidly approached the mothership and docked.
