I was sitting in a motor boat with a male and female friend travelling down a long river with cropland full of wheat to the left and a elevated forest to the right. I was holding a GoPro to film our journey when I overheard lots of rustling leaves from the forest.

> **Me**: Does anyone hear that?
> 

> **Woman**: Hear what?
> 

I turned around to see lots of people dressed in grey medieval full plate armour emerge as different groups moved cannons on wheels to the front as the rest stood idly in a line at the back watching everything.

> **Me**: What do you think this is all about?
> 

The knights ignited the cannons with flaming firebrand torches and attempted to aim them at us but they were highly inaccurate. Multiple cannonballs whizzed past us at different times, striking the water nearby and causing large splashes that rained droplets on us.

> **Man**: Speed up! Speed up!
> 

We set the boat to full speed and continued up the river until we evaded them. Eventually, we arrived behind my unit complex and disembarked at the side of my garage.

We briefly entered my unit when a male stranger randomly opened the front door and trespassed inside.

> **Me**: Excuse me, who are you?
> 

> **Me**: Get out!
> 

The stranger quickly left.

> **Man**: We need to report these crimes to the authorities.
> 

We travelled to the nearest police station where we reported the incidents and sat down with a detective in a room as we rewatched the footage being projected onto a whiteboard at the front.
