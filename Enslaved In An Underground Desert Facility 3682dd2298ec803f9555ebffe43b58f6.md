I was one of many people forced into slave labour in an underground desert facility ruled by a female dictator. She required daily exposure to sunlight to recharge otherwise her body would experience immense pain and slowly self-destruct. I spectated her sitting on her throne inside a large cave with servants scattered around:

- above:
    - a large opening to the surface sealed by a rotating metal dome built into the walls
- right:
    - a wooden rectangular table with a small-scale model made of sandstone showing two cities on the surface separated by a large gap
    - a hand crank mounted on the wall to rotate the dome.

I instinctively knew that we were located beneath one city which was under her control and the other city was a sanctuary. As her symptoms worsened, she called on a servant.

> **Woman**: *Groan*
> 

> **Woman**: Start letting in sunlight!
> 

> **Woman**: Quickly!
> 

They rotated the hand crank which slowly rotated the dome to reveal a slit that allowed sunlight to directly shine on her.

I was walking through a grey metallic futuristic hallway with glass panels on both sides revealing rooms when I overheard other teenagers discussing their plan to escape.

> **Man**: If we can enter the chamber when nobody is around and open the dome we should have enough time to escape.
> 

> **Me**: Do you have sufficient food and water to make the journey?
> 

> **Man**: Yeah, that would be good to factor in…
>
