I was standing outside a shopping centre looking at my reflection in the glass panels of a tech store when I noticed a grey alien standing behind me that I instinctively knew was trying to kill me. I fled into the shopping centre, ascended the escalators, and sought refuge at the back of a tall rectangular stone structure with a narrow path on both sides as five people stood idly.

> **Me**: I need help!
> 

> **Me**: An alien is following me wherever I go and trying to kill me!
> 

The alien now resembled a man with grey skin and emerged from behind. When everyone stood beside me and looked at the alien it’s legs melted into a white puddle and it fell backwards dead.
