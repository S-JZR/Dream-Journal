I was in a multiplayer videogame PVP duel with guns in a pitch-black room with an illuminated centre featuring wide rectangular concrete blocks as tall as a person arranged with equal gaps in a circular formation. There were different playstyles including:

- shooting between the gaps
- sneaking around the outside
- hiding around blocks
- climbing on top of blocks
- rushing through the middle but you would probably get shot.

I felt a bit paranoid because I had to check multiple angles simultaneously if I lost track of my opponent.
