# Description

I was spectating various robots resembling walking walls full of ammo with two miniguns on their chests and shoulder mounted rocket launchers walking down a hilly forest and assaulting a Sci-Fi base.

A smooth humanoid robot inside the base held a gun that rapidly burst fired hundreds of rounds at the head of a different thick robot with dual machine guns for arms and a shoulder mounted rocket launcher as its head exploded.

## Influences
