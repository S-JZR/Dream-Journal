# Description

I was in Minecraft with Mercedes and Gabbie, my cousins, but we were our real selves instead of avatars as we stood on a dirt plain with a grid of trees that had a mound of dirt across each row. The rows further in the distance had more obstructions on them including barb wire fences and random objects. I installed a mod that added General Corrosive from Metal Arms who started chasing them around. I hid behind a tree and shot a flare into the sky to try distracting him but it didn’t work. I confronted him and yelled loudly then he started chasing me.

> **Me**: Hey over here!
> 

I fled through the hills and climbed between the barb wire fences as he got closer when I decided running away was ridiculous because I was in control of the dream and teleported back to my cousins. As General Corrosive was approaching I instantly stretched out a giant brick wall that stopped him. We released an energy ball that killed him and knocked him onto his back. As I climbed onto his chest to see if he was dead I was electrocuted by a chest trap.
