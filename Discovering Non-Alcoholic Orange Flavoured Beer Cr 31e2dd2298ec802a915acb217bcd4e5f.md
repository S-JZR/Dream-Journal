# Description

I was browsing items in a small 7-Eleven convenience store when I discovered non-alcoholic orange flavoured beer bottles created by Optus which I thought was strange and carried them in my arms. After standing at the front of the store for a long time without buying anything the female cashier was annoyed.

> **Female 1**: Excuse me you need to buy something or leave.
> 

> **Me**: Can’t I just keep browsing?
> 

She immediately picked up a phone on the counter and called the local police department.

> **Female 1**: Hello police there is someone who is refusing to buy anything and preventing customers from entering the store.
> 

I placed the bottles on the counter and paid for them.

> **Me**: Alright, I’ll buy them.
> 

A male police officer arrived to investigate but didn’t see anything wrong and the cashier covered for me as she waved him away.

> **Policeman**: Is everything alright, have they gone?
> 

> **Female 1**: Yes, it’s all fine now.
> 

I returned to my apartment room which was directly beside a store in the hallway. I noticed my beers were warm so I entered the store with the intent to leave them in a freezer and retrieve them later. However, I didn’t want to look like a shoplifter and decided not to do it. As I turned around to leave a distant female employee noticed and thought I was shoplifting anyway.

> **Female 2**: Hey, do you have a receipt for those?
> 

> **Me**: Yes, I bought them from another store earlier.
> 

She approached me and I showed her the receipt. I returned to my apartment to drink a beer but I was disappointed to discover it merely tasted like ordinary orange juice.
