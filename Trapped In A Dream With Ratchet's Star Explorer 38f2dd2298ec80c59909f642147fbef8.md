I was standing in a tall square concrete area:

- top: smooth wall
- left: semi-circular concrete tunnel with a two-lane road and a parallel light strip on the ceiling
- bottom: semi-circular concrete tunnel (the same design)
- right: semi-circular concrete tunnel (the same design)
- centre: circular launch pad with Ratchet’s brown Star Explorer from Ratchet And Clank 3 bobbing on it
- above: no roof and a blue sky with some clouds.

I briefly walked through the left tunnel but noticed it stretched for an unknown distance and returned. I realized that I was dreaming and was afraid that I would be trapped there forever because I couldn’t wake up. Eventually, the dream ended and I woke up.
