# Description

I was part of a police unit dispatched to a distress call on an island with a skyscraper beside coastal cities. Upon arriving at a long textured stone pier with an awning and loose rocks on both sides, a man behind a distant art installation opened fire on us. We took cover behind nearby red metal poles as bullets struck them repeatedly. While taking turns to return fire, I was hit in the shoe, severing my toe, and I accidentally pulled the trigger too early as another bullet ricocheted off the pole into my leg.
