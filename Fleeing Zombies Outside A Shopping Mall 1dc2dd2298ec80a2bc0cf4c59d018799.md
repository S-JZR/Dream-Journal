I was exiting a shopping mall in the USA into a carpark towards a spot beside the main road with a four-layer strip of boulders. A bearded dragon climbed a boulder on the first-layer at the front and hid inside a crevice. A bald elderly man with a white beard ascended through the top layer of boulders via a deep ladder emerging from the sewers.

> **Man**: Flee as far as you can!
> 

> **Man**: Zombies have appeared in Washington and are infecting everyone across the country!
> 

A horde of zombies walked across the entire right side of the the carpark towards me. I fled to a nearby multi-storey building to the left and ascended to the top floor where I called my mum.

> **Me**: [Relaying the situation]!
> 

> **Me**: Buy as many packs of water and cans of soup as possible and have them delivered to us so we can wait out the apocalypse!
> 

> **Mum**: How do you even know any of this is real?
> 

> **Mum**: I don’t want to waste money buying all of that stuff.
>
