# Description

I was one of many people at a harbour attending the opening ceremony of a new next generation cruise ship. Almost all corporations and small businesses invested in the project and it featured the largest shopping mall ever built on a ship. After boarding, the ship was reported to be slowly sinking so it was lifted back onto the harbour by cranes while everyone was still onboard. All travelling was suspended until further notice until the problem was fixed but everyone was welcome to stay. While walking past a pharmacy, I noticed that most stores discounted their products by around 50% because they were losing money from a decline in customers and desperate to sell everything. I bought a cheap tiny $20 flip phone to call my mum.

> **Me**: Do you want anything from the pharmacy because most stores have discounted everything?
> 

> **Mum**: Is there a problem, why are they discounted?
> 

> **Me**: [Relaying the situation].
> 

The call kept disconnecting and experiencing interference so I couldn't talk about anything else.
