I was walking down the hallway of a general practitioner clinic with two rooms featuring a doctor and patient sitting on opposite sides of a desk engaged in a consultation. On each desk was a dark-grey-green rectangular wooden block covered on one side with 2 inch nails in a grid formation.

In the first room, I noticed an invisible ghost pulling out all of the nails simultaneously as they levitated on the desk and rotated them towards the patient with the intent to launch them into their arm. I hurriedly slid a rectangular piece of yellow ballistics gel in the way as the nails deeply embedded themselves inside.

In the second room, I checked in to see if everything was fine but noticed the same phenomena occurring again and used a book to block the nails.
