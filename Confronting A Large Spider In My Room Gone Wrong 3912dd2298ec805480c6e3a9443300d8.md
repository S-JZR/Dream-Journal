I was sitting at my computer while my brother was at his in our room in my unit, when I noticed a 15cm green spider with thin limbs motionless on the front face of a clock atop a shelf in front of me.

> **Me**: There’s a huge spider on the clock!
> 

> **Me**: Go downstairs and get some bug spray!
> 

While my brother was descending the stairs I was afraid and kept track of the spider from outside the room. I didn’t realize there were three lines of web on the ceiling that stretched to different points in the room and the spider used one of them to rapidly swing in front of the doorway and I fled. When my brother return he sprayed the spider with a can of Mortein but it ran beneath the bunk bed with lots of items stored under it.

> **Me**: Where did it go?
> 

> **Brother**: It ran away.
> 

> **Me**: Where did it run away to?
> 

> **Brother**: It went under the bed.
> 

> **Brother**: It had 5 babies on its back.
> 

I went downstairs to grab a can of Mortein with a nozzle and began spraying the perimeter of the room behind furniture and atop objects under the bed so it couldn't secretly move around. I did a few quick bursts on electrical cables because I was worried it could catch fire.
