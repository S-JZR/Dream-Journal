I was standing in a safehouse connected to an underground carpark with other survivors as they discussed an escape route. I instinctively knew that we were in an apocalypse where the world was overrun by prehistoric creatures that attacked all light sources. I went downstairs to a door with a window leading to the carpark and peeled back newspapers covering it to shine a flashlight outside where I saw an Arthropleura twisting around ceiling pipes and a white velociraptor, resembling an animal seen through night vision. The velociraptor aggressively approached the door to peer inside and a black man behind me sprinted to put back the newspapers and warned me to refrain from looking outside again.
