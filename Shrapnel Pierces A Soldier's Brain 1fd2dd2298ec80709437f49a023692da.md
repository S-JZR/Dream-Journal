# Description

A timid male soldier was cowering among crates in a small shed missing its back half some distance behind the frontline when an officer approached to order him to advance forward. However, an artillery shell unexpectedly landed nearby and a metal fragment about 0.5cm wide ricocheted off the ground, boring a hole upward through the soldierâ€™s nasal skin, and lodging slightly in his brain. He panicked at the sensation of the hot metal, fearing it would leave him disabled and require surgery.
