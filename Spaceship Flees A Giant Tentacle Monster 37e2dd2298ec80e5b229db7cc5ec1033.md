I was a crew member standing beside the cockpit of a spaceship flying through space with a fleet as we were suddenly ambushed by a giant black tentacle monster. The pilot hurriedly tried to get away safely as staff talked with each other in a calm and serious tone. I overheard a distant explosion.

> **Female Radio Operator**: Alpha was hit and all crew are dead.
> 

> **Male Chief Engineer**: Divert power to the back shields.
> 

> **Female Co-pilot**: Switch to evasive manoeuvres.
> 

I couldn't see anything out the front window but the ship was suddenly clipped by the edge of a tentacle as everything violently shook.

We successfully got away from the monster but various machinery was damaged as they sparked and wires and cracked pipes were exposed from the ceiling.

> **Male Captain**: Investigate the damage and repair everything.
> 

The spaceship was equipped with engineer robots on monowheels that could build anything. I walked past the dark server room and noticed crew members deploying the robots to weld everything back together. I thought about exploiting the opportunity to use a spare robot to fabricate a new phone for me but decided it was best to help out and prepare the spaceship for another emergency.
