# Description

I was attending a fancy banquet for memorial day. The room was spacious with many tables arranged in concentric ovals around a central flat wooden dance floor. The tables featured a white silk cloth, golden cutlery, wine glasses, flowers, and entrees. I was sitting on the outer ring facing the wall beside Noah and Reece, students I knew from primary school.

> **Man**: We honour the lives of the brave men and women who sacrificed their lives to give us the freedom we experience today.
> 

While the speaker was delivering a message, Noah and Reece were loudly arguing over petty things which disturbed everyone who was looking at them. I continued eating casually as if nothing was happening.

> **Noah**: You're supposed to eat the bread roll first!
> 

> **Reece**: Don't tell me what to do, I'll do what I want!
> 

> **Noah**: Well sorry that you can’t even do the basics!
> 

The speaker was annoyed and politely reprimanded them as they settled down.

> **Man**: Can those at the back stop talking please.
> 

Eventually, I entered a nearby side bathroom and sat in a bathroom stall to do a number 1. Someone who was busting to do a number 2 walked in and I saw the shadows of their feet on the floor.

> **Man 2**: How long are you going to be because I really need to go.
> 

> **Me**: I’ll try to be as quick as I can.
> 

> **Man 2**: I’m struggling to hold it in!
> 

I figured that I didn’t need to be in there and could swap to the urinal.

> **Me**: I can swap to the urinal and you can come in.
> 

> **Man 2**: Thanks man.
>
