I was one of many secondary students attending a graduation formal at a former local church where a DJ using a DJ console onstage displayed music videos on a wide pull-down projector screen mounted on the ceiling. I don’t remember any song lyrics. Most people were dancing in front of the stage and scattered apart but others were still seated watching everyone. I had to use a fictional bathroom at the back built on metal bleachers and sat on the toilet doing a #2 as I watched the event through a crack in the door. I felt bad that I was missing out and tried to wipe my butt clean faster but there was always little bits of poo leftover. Eventually, The DJ put on a trendy song from a fictional boyband that was unanimously popular and those who were seated got up to dance. Everyone moved forward to be tightly bunched together and danced more enthusiastically.
