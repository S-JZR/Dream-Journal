I was ascending stairs onto an H-shaped underpass, beneath a highway and above a road with cars moving past, to cross over to the other side. A single metal door was in the top-left and top-right corner and straight stairs were in the bottom-left and bottom-right corner. While crossing the middle, I noticed there were about three large spiders motionless on the left railings and ten on the wall to the right of the door ahead. I was freaked out and rushed to descend the stairs so I could get away.
