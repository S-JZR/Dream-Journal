# Description

I was one of many adults equipped with machine guns playing a wave-based FPS arcade game where we stood behind a bench in a videogame store with a blue carpet. We shot terminators as they walked through a wide open rectangular door on the opposite side of the room. A boy was talking to another boy at the left side of the room before walking to the right in front of everyone’s line of sight. I warned everyone in chat not to harm him because he was on our side.
