I was playing a videogame as I stood in a snowy mountain base with an army when robots invaded the planet and we had to clear them out. I opened a unit powerup menu styled like a fantasy medieval mobile game and saw a castle in the top right and five stair pathways criss-crossing between five circular platforms that I could activate to emit an energy bubble and place five units inside to powerup their abilities. I exited the front of the mountain on elevated terrain and struggled to drag a heavy mini cannon on wheels across the ground to the other side of large pit and up stairs. There was also a flat concrete garden retainer on the right side with plants that I could of more easily moved across to the top step but chose not to. I went back in time and purchased a strength upgrade that allowed me to easily carry the cannon on my shoulders.

I transformed into a smooth grey robot and everyone gathered at a huge chasm dividing the map into two halves. I instinctively knew that the robots wanted a challenger or a few people to participate in a challenge to grant us access to cross the chasm. A few levitating platforms and a large red/purple surfboard platform ascended. I walked onto the surfboard which moved in circles and fought off oncoming drones. I went back in time and redid it knowing what to expect and showed off athletically as I perfectly timed jumping and front flipping on the platforms and controlled the surfboard to go in circles. A man was angry that I was focusing on an easy problem.

> **Man**: Why don’t you go seal the tunnels in the mountains so no more enemies can infiltrate us?
> 

I looked at the mini map and saw a long tunnel in the mountains with five circles on it representing the tunnel entrances and five green dots of units already there. leftmost side of the mountains and let everyone else resolve the challenge. All of the tunnels curved downwards. I saw two snowy tunnels close together and a man repeatedly patting a mysterious pattern carved in stone on the bottom of the one to the right which slowly lifted a wall made of snow to seal it. I instinctively knew we had to learn what the symbols were elsewhere to perform these actions but we already happened to know. I went to the one on the left and brushed off the snow to find the symbol and repeated the process. As the man finished he stood beside me watching.

> **Me**: You know we could speed this up if you hit it with me.
> 

> **Man**: It’s fine don’t worry about it.
> 

I moved deeper into a pitch black cavern with the man and went in a circle when a robot bat with a red light flew inside and circled on the ceiling. I had a hard time seeing it and pointed at it as a yellow line was drawn in that direction from the centre of the minimap. I was only able to sprint or deploy a purple forcefield shield at one time. I tried backing up to distance myself from the bat but it dive bombed me so I deployed the shield and it died when striking the front. Every robot I killed gave me more parts to upgrade myself.

I travelled further down the tunnels where I discovered additional tunnels led to a cavern inhabited by hostile yetis and they wrapped one tunnel in a plastic bag which they used as a trash bin.

At the other side of the tunnel I encountered a table and was ambushed by small drones with a red light and a pipe weapon beneath them that exploded on contact. I moved behind the table and they tried to ram into me individually but I killed the drones by whacking them to the ground with a stick. I purchased a x10 CPU upgrade which made me run and perform actions twice as fast. I went back in time to the tunnels to do them faster.

A cutscene played showing a distress call from a spaceship being attacked by a Zorgon spaceship from Zethaura.

> **Woman**: Turn off all equipment so there are no lights.
> 

Everything was turned off on the ship and the Zorgon’s left but in doing so all the robots onboard went rogue and were attacking everyone so we had to rescue the crew trapped in the bridge.

> **Woman**: Requesting an emergency evacuation, we’re being attacked by aliens and robots onboard have gone rogue.
> 

The sky was dark red/brown and a smooth grey rectangular dropship landed and opened a back ramp as I had to load 150 units onboard. While sending units I was walking with a high ranking male NPC with lots of chest medals that I became intimate with by casually discussing playing Warzone 2100 recently. However, we took too long and by the time we got to the dropship the last unit was about to walk inside.

> **Me**: Wait no no no!
> 

We sprinted towards the back but it entered halfway through a black trigger wall and everything faded to white as the level completed. I didn’t want to continue without the NPC I liked and not have his skill contributing so I went back in time and made sure he was loaded onboard.

We landed in the spaceship hub with lines on the floor as robots moving around everywhere. I transformed into a human again and went with Julien, my friend from primary school, to sneak to elevators. One elevator went to the top half of the spaceship and the other went to the bottom half. However, when opening the elevator four hostile humanoid robots resembling David Tennant exited. We lost two by running around in a circle but when we came back two were still standing in front of the elevators. I was able to return them to their default mode by asking for help.

> **Me**: There are crew members trapped in the bridge who are fighting for their lives.
> 

> **Me**: Can you help us?
> 

> **Robots**: Lead the way.
> 

While we were about to enter the elevator with them I had second thoughts and let them go up by themselves as the doors closed.

> **Julien**: What’s wrong?
> 

> **Me**: I don’t want to ride with defective robots in an elevator that could turn on us again.
> 

> **Me**: We would be trapped inside with nowhere to go.
> 

We snuck around to find other elevators and found some but other robots saw us and we lost them by running in a circle but I also lost Julien. The elevators didn’t go to the bridge. I snuck around and found a woman.

> **Me**: I’m trying to find a way to safely get to the bridge.
> 

> **Me**: Can you help me?
> 

> **Woman**: Sure, follow me.
> 

We entered a spacious elevator on the exterior of the spaceship with a large glass window on the front. We sat down in two chairs in the centre and strapped ourselves in.

> **Woman**: Brace yourself we’re about to experience 10Gs.
> 

The elevator ascended rapidly across the spaceship and I closed my eyes to avoid being overwhelmed by things rapidly moving past that I thought I wouldn’t be able to process. Eventually, we arrived on the floor of the bridge.
