# Description

I was standing in the backyard of my unit where we had a magical pot plant that grew steaks like leaves. I brought a steak into the kitchen to prepare it when a fictional woman knocked on the front door so I let her inside.

> **Woman**: Are you busy?
> 

> **Me**: No, I was just getting meat from the backyard.
> 

I realized that sounded strange and didn’t want her to think I was a serial killer.

> **Me**: We have a magical plant in the backyard that grows meat.
> 

I thought it would be best to show her the plant through the kitchen window instead of taking her into the backyard to avoid making her feel like she was being led into a trap.

> **Me**: You can look at it through the kitchen window if you want.
> 

> **Woman**: Sure.
>
