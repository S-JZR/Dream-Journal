# Description

I was standing inside my unit at night when I overheard consecutive abnormally loud thunder claps and everyone in the unit complex exited their units to investigate.

> **Woman**: What is going on?
> 

> **Man 1**: I don’t know.
> 

> **Man 2**: Does anyone know what that noise is?
> 

I saw a large bright light appear in the sky and specific people around the world were being raptured at different times by transforming into blue balls of energy and ascending towards the light. I was afraid of what it might feel like to be raptured and closed my eyes in case I was but nothing happened and I was left behind. After the event finished, the world descended into chaos from societal collapse. I noticed my religious parents were still present and wondered how perfect we were supposed to be to get raptured because even my parents couldn’t make it.

> **Me**: Why do you think God didn’t rapture you?
> 

> **Mum**: Who knows, God works in mysterious ways, and maybe he still has a plan for us to stay here and keep doing his will.
> 

I noticed that a group of people were systematically going up the street and looting different homes that were empty or they outnumbered.

> **Me**: We should gather all of our valuables and burry them so nobody can steal them.
> 

> **Mum**: I still need to pay some bills and rent, I’ll be back later.
> 

> **Me**: Mum you need to take off all of your jewellery otherwise you could be attacked.
> 

I returned to the kitchen and brought her a small square plastic container full of fried chicken.

> Me: Take this in case you get stuck out there because you may not find anything to eat.
> 

> Mum: Good idea.
> 

The rest of my family returned inside and I locked the front fly screen and wooden door for our protection.
