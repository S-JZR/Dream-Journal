# Description

I was a criminal with a partner in a videogame trying to steal as much as possible from people’s houses without being seen otherwise the level immediately ended. Each resident constantly moved positions to perform different activities and knew when critical things were missing so it was necessary to steal them in specific orders and cause distractions to move them away. Critical missing items were marked with a yellow warning eye symbol and box outline. One of the tasks was collecting water from taps which took a long time. I had a handheld radar scanner that showed anyone who was moving nearby as green dots but this also included my partner so communication was important.
