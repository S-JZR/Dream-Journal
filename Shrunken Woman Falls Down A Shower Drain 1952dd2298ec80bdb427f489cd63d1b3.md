# Shrunken Woman Falls Down A Shower Drain

is_favourite: No
is_nsfw: Yes
date: January 1, 2005
updated_at: July 13, 2026 3:00 AM
type: Nightmare
emotion: Fear
influences: The Incredible Shrinking Man (1957): I think I remember seeing snippets of the main character fighting off a giant spider as a child when someone was flicking through TV channels which made me feel unsettled and worried about his safety.
symbols: Shrinking: The fear of being helpless.

I was spectating a woman in an old greyscale horror film taking a shower in a white alcove bathtub with white curtains when lightning bolts emitted from the showerhead struck her and she shrank to 1 inch tall. Different shots showed her moving down the drain from a high-angle close-up of the hole and an external side view of the pipe. She was in a falling pose on her back with one arm raised and the other down. The shot was overlaid with the eerie waving title “Down The Drain”.
