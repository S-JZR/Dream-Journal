I was spectating a blond astronaut travelling through space in an egg-shaped camouflaged spaceship with four landing legs, trying to reach a destination. The entire time, he was in a radio call with other crew members at a remote HQ as the signal relayed across a chain of distant satellites. After accidentally getting lost, he encountered a red, green, and two grey planets that displayed on internal monitors. The green planet showed excessive signs of life covering the entire surface when scanned. Upon landing, it was discovered that Creepers from Minecraft had bred uncontrollably and covered the surface to conceal the grey stone beneath. The loud noise of the rocket scared them away into various nearby cave entrances where they couldn’t completely fit inside but eventually came out again to curiously surround it as he observed them through the hemisphere windows.

> **AI**: Don’t open the door until I can confirm they are not a threat.
> 

> **Man 1**: Requesting backup to be on standby in case I need to be evacuated.
> 

> **Man 2**: Copy that, standing by.
> 

> **AI**: My scan indicates that it's safe to leave.
> 

A hostile faction discovered the astronaut was on the planet and dispatched a dropship that deployed a male dark jedi wearing black clothes, chains, and wielding a red lightsabre to assassinate him. After fleeing across a series of mountains, white robot lazer sentries scattered across them recognised a human in distress and opened fire at the dark jedi who was significantly delayed by reflecting the shots back to destroy them.

> **Man 1**: I'm under hostile pursuit and request an immediate evacuation.
> 

He trespassed into a large restricted white ring-shaped storage facility resembling Apple HQ. The outer rim featured a wide illuminated hallway with a grey carpet and panelled windows on both sides. The inner rim featured a warehouse with thousands of tall metal shelves with rectangular dark grey storage boxes. A levitating white disc robot with a sniper rifle underneath patrolled the hallway and immediately opened fire at him. While fleeing, he discovered a male survivor hiding among shelves in the hallway.

> **Survivor**: Please rescue me, I’ve been trapped here for days!
> 

> **Man 1**: I will try to find a safe route to get us out of here.
> 

As a rounded white rectangular vehicle robot with headlights, a gun, and a scanner approached from the other side, they entered the dark warehouse, where many others were patrolling. When the robot came near, they opened two empty rectangular storage containers on the bottom shelves and climbed inside to lay down motionless. The containers opened from the sides and had an illuminated internal strip that activated each time they were opened. The robot scanned their bodies with a blue triangular beam and continued moving.

After exiting the facility through a fire exit, they ascending to the roof as other crew arrived, one of which was Carter from Rush Hour, wearing fully-body dark grey armour. He sassily tried to reason with the AI system by talking to a sniper turret on a distant roof.

> Carter: Hey! Tin can! I know you can hear me!
> 

Five separate shots, each with a brief pause between them, struck his chest plate and helmet, knocking him back repeatedly but he kept moving back in defiance.

> **Carter**: Is that all you got b***h ass piece of junk!
> 

> **Carter**: Come on hit me again I dare you!
> 

> **Man 1**: (Radio) What the hell are you doing?!
> 

> **Man 1**: (Radio) Stop standing in the open!
> 

> **Man 1**: (Radio) Are you trying to get yourself killed?!
> 

Carter listened and moved out of sight behind roof structures.

As the astronaut and survivor descended the stairs to cross a large flat outdoor loading bay, the dark jedi emerged from behind to chase them. The survivor was fatally stabbed in the chest and the astronaut tripped onto the ground then looked back.

> **Man 1**: Please don't kill me!
> 

> **Dark Jedi**: I sense darkness in you.
> 

> **Dark Jedi**: Will you join the dark side?
