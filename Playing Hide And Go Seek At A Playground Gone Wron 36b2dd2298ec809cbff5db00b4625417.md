# Description

I was one of many people of ages ranging from children to teenagers playing hide and go seek at a playground supervised by secondary school teachers who were seated on park benches across the road. The playground had a black metal fence and a power pole inside to one side. A group of teenagers sought to create an unreachable hiding spot atop the power pole by haphazardly hammering wooden planks onto one side to create a makeshift ladder and climbing up. One male teenager was tightrope walking across the power lines to another power pole while balancing a stack of long wooden planks. Everyone noticed him and was concerned that he might fall or conduct electricity by touching two wires with the planks and a female teacher yelled at him.

> **Woman**: Get down from there!
> 

> **Woman**: You could kill yourself!
> 

Another male teacher drove a fire truck closer with the intent to raise the ladder to help everyone get down but accidentally drove through the fence and hit the power pole which toppled over. Everyone on the power pole fell down onto the road and suffered severe injuries. As ambulances and paramedics arrived, I noticed people standing around the teenager who was previously atop the power lines lying in a foetal position on the road and approached to hear him crying out in pain.

> **Man**: It hurts so much!
> 

> **Man**: I want to die!
>
