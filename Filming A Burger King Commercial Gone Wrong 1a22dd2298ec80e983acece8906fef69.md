I was one of many actors portraying scientists in a burger king commercial that used a vlog style. We were conducting a secret investigation to determine whether the burgers were truly better than other brands. The footage cut between shots of the store from afar and the last one zoomed in on customers entering the front door. We gathered samples and concluded that 7 out of 10 people preferred Burger King. However, while standing in the parking lot in front of the store we soon realized that we accidentally filmed at a McDonald's.
