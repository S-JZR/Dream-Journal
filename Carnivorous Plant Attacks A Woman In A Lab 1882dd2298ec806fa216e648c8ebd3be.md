# Description

I was spectating a fictional first-person 2D horror video game with a female lab staff member who entered a hallway to confront a giant carnivorous plant swaying in an idle animation.

> **Male Colleague (Radio)**: What are you doing? Get out of there!
**Woman**: …
> 

She hesitated for a few seconds and the plant lunged at her without warning, 

> **Woman**: Ahh!
> 

Everything instantly faded to black and silence.
