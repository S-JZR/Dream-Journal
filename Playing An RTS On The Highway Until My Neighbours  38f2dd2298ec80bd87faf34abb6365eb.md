I was playing an RTS videogame against some fictional teenage neighbours of both sexes. I built a line of spaced Ripple Rocket Batteries on a highway beside stores as cars drove past and kept going until it spanned multiple kilometres all the way to their house. The terrain transitioned into a grand canyon halfway along the highway with a wall of mountains on both sides and was devoid of cars. There was also a basin to the left with two entrances that was covered in a fog of war. The road abruptly ended on an elevated cliff overlooking the house where my neighbours lived in a grassy neighbourhood.

They sent 20 armoured tanks across the highway to try moving past the defences but they were all destroyed by the long range missiles that simultaneously hit them. Eventually, nothing was sent for a long time and I realized that my neighbours gave up. When I approached their house, I saw them jumping on a trampoline with an enclosure in their backyard through a chain-link fence.
