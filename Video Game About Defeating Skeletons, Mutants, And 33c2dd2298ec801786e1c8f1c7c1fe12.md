# Description

I was playing a videogame with multiple players about journeying through an Egyptian pyramid and were equipped with flare guns that had limited ammo. We travelled through an angled C-shaped hallway with smooth 50 metre tall walls when three enemy skeletons emerged in the distance. We shot multiple flares at them but they merely bounced off and didn’t ignite them. One skeleton proceeded to attack a man to my right.

> **Man 1**: I need some help!
> 

I turned around to shoot the skeleton in the back which finally ignited it and we killed them all. In the final chamber we encountered a mutant boss with eight arms and repeatedly shot flares at it until it died.

To the right of the chamber, a hoard of zombies were breaking through boxes covering a doorway. We hid in a side room and boarded it up with wooden planks because we didn’t want to fight them with low ammo. We looked through a rectangular tunnel at floor level in the wall to see another group of survivors following the previous hallway.

> **Me**: Hey, we’re low on ammo!
> 

> **Me**: Can you help us?
> 

> **Man 2**: Sure, take this.
> 

The other group slid machine guns and ammo through the tunnel to us and we removed the boards to fight the zombies.
