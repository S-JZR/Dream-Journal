# Cockroaches Infest A Tall Building

is_nsfw: No
date: January 14, 2025
updated_at: June 5, 2026 7:39 PM
type: Nightmare
emotion: Fear
influences: Men In Black (1997): Likely references the Annelid aliens in the kitchen.
lessons: Don’t blindly follow the crowd.

I was standing with a crowd in the open space on the second highest floor of a multi-story concrete building with parapets between square pillars along the perimeter. After mingling for some time, cockroaches began raining down from the roof onto the floor, causing everyone to move away in panic. Some of us went to the stairs but they were were also infested with cockroaches crawling out from the lower levels. Many ascended to the top floor where they climbed over the parapets and proceeded to the end of a large L-shaped roof made of corrugated metal. However, after those in front, including myself, realized it led to a sharp drop we immediately tuned back and warned everyone.

> **People**: Go back, it’s a dead end!
> 

> **People**: You’re going to push us off the side!
> 

Noticing many short and tall blue plastic buckets stacked against a central wall on the previous level, I filled one with water to wash away the cockroaches on the lower staircase. A father and son were also trying to get a bucket and he was annoyed when I took a smaller one.

> **Me**: You can take the larger ones, I don’t need them.
> 

After teaming up with two men and two women, we descended into a hallway on the lower level and entered a side room divided by a green translucent glass wall and door covered in detailed instructions about how to handle acid. We encountered two evolved cockroaches standing 30 centimetres tall as one of them insulted everyone and spat drops of green acid onto the shirts of the women who were closest.

> **Cockroach**: You humans are pathetic and think you run the place.
> 

> **Cockroach**: You disgust me.
> 

> **Cockroach**: *spit*
> 

> **Woman**: Don’t do that again!
> 

> **Cockroach:** *spit*
> 

One of the women was enraged and shot it in the head with a pistol as it lifelessly fell to the ground. I offered emergency advice to the women.

> **Me**: You need to dab your shirts with water to dilute the acid!
> 

> **Me**: Don't soak it or the acid will touch your skin.
> 

After hearing rumours that this entire incident was an experiment gone wrong, one of the men visited the house of an elderly woman associated with the project a few blocks away. He knocked on the door to question her but she seemed crazy.

> **Man**: What do you know about the lab project?
> 

> **Woman**: ...
> 

She shut the door then slid a card under it with the alphabet written on six lines and a few letters marked with dotted underlines.
